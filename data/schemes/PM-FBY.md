Scheme Name:Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
State/Central: Central Government Scheme, Implemented jointly by Central + State Governments
Target Audience: Farmers cultivating notified crops (small, marginal, loanee & non-loanee farmers)

Objective of PMFBY:
* Provide financial protection to farmers against crop loss
* Stabilize farmer income
* Encourage use of modern agricultural practices

 Land Ownership
* Farmers growing notified crops in notified areas
* Includes:
    * Loanee farmers (compulsory earlier, now optional)
    * Non-loanee farmers
* Must have insurable interest in the crop

 Age Limit
*  No specific age limit mentioned
* Must be an eligible farmer cultivating crops

 Crop Type
* Applicable to notified crops only, such as:
    * Food crops (rice, wheat, etc.)
    * Oilseeds
    * Commercial / horticultural crops

 Citizenship
* Must be an Indian farmer

Exclusions (Who Cannot Apply)
* Farmers not growing notified crops
* Farmers outside notified areas
* Cases where:
    * Wrong information is provided
    * Premium is not paid

 Financial Benefits
 Insurance Coverage
* Provides crop insurance coverage against:
    * Natural calamities (drought, flood, cyclone)
    * Pests and diseases

 Low Premium Rates
Farmers pay very low premium:
* 2% for Kharif crops
* 1.5% for Rabi crops
* 5% for commercial/horticulture crops
 Remaining premium is subsidized by Government

 Compensation Coverage
* Prevented sowing
* Standing crop loss
* Post-harvest losses
* Localized disasters (hailstorm, landslide, etc.)

Required Documents
Identity Proof
* Aadhaar card (preferred)

 Land / Crop Details
* Land records or proof of cultivation
* Crop sown details

 Bank Details
* Bank account number
* IFSC code

 Additional
* Sowing certificate (if required)
* Insurance proposal form
