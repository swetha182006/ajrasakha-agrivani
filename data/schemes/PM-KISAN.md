Scheme Name : Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Target Audience: The PM-KISAN portal is designed only for a single central sector scheme. That scheme provides ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmers in 3 installments.

Objective: Income Support to Farmers
Reduce Financial Burden
Ensure Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Eligibility Criteria: Land Ownership: Must be a landholding farmer family, Land should be registered in the farmer’s name as per state/UT land records 
Age Limit : No specific age limit mentioned in the scheme, Beneficiary must be part of a farmer family (adult members) 
Crop Type : No restriction on crop type, Scheme applies to all types of cultivable land, regardless of crops grown 
Citizenship :Must be an Indian citizen ,Must belong to a farmer family owning cultivable land in India

Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply): Institutional Landholders 
Government Employees & Officials
Professionals (High-income category) 
Income Tax Payers
Constitutional Post Holders

Financial Benefits: ₹6,000 per year per eligible farmer family Paid in 3 equal installments: ₹2,000 each Given every 4 months Payments are made every year, subject to eligibility verification

Required documents:
1)Identity Proof (Mandatory) Aadhaar Number (preferred) If Aadhaar not available: Aadhaar Enrollment Number OR, Any valid ID such as:Voter ID, Driving License, NREGA Job Card, Other government-issued ID
2)Bank Account Details: Bank Account Number, IFSC Code, Account must be in the beneficiary’s name, Used for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
3)Mobile Number Required for: SMS alerts , Payment updates, (Optional but recommended)
4)Land Records : Proof of ownership of cultivable land, Verified through State/UT land records system
5)Personal Details: Name, Gender, Category (SC/ST, etc.), Address , Age/Date of Birth (optional)
6)Self-Declaration : Declaration confirming:Eligibility, Not falling under exclusion categories Used by State/UT for verification
